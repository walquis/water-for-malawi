# GG2688284 - Current Global Grant

## Water for Life - Malawi

Leveraging the success and experience gained from the previous global water grant, we are seeking to drill five sustainable wells this summer
in the Zomba region of Malawi.

VIP will again be our NGO partner, and Blantyre will again serve as the host club.

## Villages to receive one or more wells
- Manduwasa (460 ppl)
- Makuluni (635 ppl)
- Mmwala (1,405 ppl)
- Maulana (890 ppl)
- Maliwata (1,135 ppl)

This project will benefit about 1,000-1,250 people across the five new wells.
