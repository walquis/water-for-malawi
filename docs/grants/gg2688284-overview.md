Expected Impact

Economic and Social Justice

A nearby well can transform the social structure of a community for the

better, especially for women and girls.

Girls no longer have to spend large portions of their day fetching water

and can go to school.

Women similarly can spend freed-up time engaging in economic

opportunities more suited to their capabilities and goals.
