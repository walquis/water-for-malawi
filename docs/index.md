# Foundation Committee of the Rotary Club of Downers Grove

The **Foundation Committee of the Rotary Club of Downers Grove** leads international service projects that bring safe water, sanitation, and hygiene to communities in Malawi.

This site shares the story of our work, the impact of past projects, and opportunities to partner with us on current and future Rotary Global Grants.

Learn more about who we are and how we work on the **About Us** page.

## Our focus in Malawi

We partner with local organizations and Rotary clubs in Malawi to:

- **Provide clean, reliable water** through boreholes and other appropriate technologies.
- **Improve sanitation and hygiene** in villages and schools.
- **Strengthen communities** through training, local ownership, and long-term sustainability planning.

Learn more about our work on the **Grants** pages, including our current project GG2688284 and our completed project GG2122731.
