Create a water for life website:

Foundation Committee of the Rotary Club of Downers Grove

Purpose: Tell the story of the work being done in Malawi by the Foundation Committee of the Rotary Club of Downers Grove

Audience: Prospective partners in future grants
- Rotary club officers and members who may want to participate and/or donate
- Potential donors outside of Rotary
- Friends who want to know what Rotary is about

Menu:
- Home - Foundation Committee of the Rotary Club of Downers Grove
  - Committee members
    - Chris Walquist - Foundation Committee Chair
    - Harry McCabe
    - Laura Trompeter
    - Randa Bishlawi
- Grants - a list of grants, in descending chronological order.
  - GG2688284 - A global grant currenty seeking approval.
  - GG2122731 - A past global grant from 2021.

Instructions:
- Drawing from the content in the source-content folder, create the website in mkdocs.
- When creating a grant page, convert the grant application's 2026-02-17-GG2688284-DRAFT-R1.pdf file to markdown using pandoc, which is installed on this mac.  This is the primary source for describing the grant.
- Add a github workflow that publishes the main and dev branches to github.
- You may use 2026-02-17 Grant Fundraising Presentation-R1.pdf for source material, but keep in mind that it's derived from a ppt for a live presentation.
- "GG2688284 Rotary Global Grant - Community Assessment Results.pdf" is source material for the 2026-02-17-GG2688284-DRAFT-R1.pdf grant application.

- For GG2122731, GG2122731-malawi-wash-final-report.docx is the final report for that grant.
